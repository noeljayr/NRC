A little information about NRC

Background

NRC is a tailored political philosophy developed exclusively for the nation of Malawi. It serves as a transformative framework aimed at driving positive change and playing a pivotal role in shaping a revitalized Malawi.

Vision

NRC envisions a Malawi where citizens' well-being surpasses human dignity, promoting active participation in financial and welfare goals, fair labor compensation, and welfare as a fundamental right.

Mission

To drive change in Malawi, prioritizing the welfare and prosperity of all citizens and maximizing the country's potential for the benefit of all.
