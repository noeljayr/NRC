Why Should You Get Involved?

This campaign is a rallying cry against age-old injustices and limitations placed on the youth. Here's why you should be a part of this transformative journey:

Empower the Youth

It's time for a generational shift, and your involvement is pivotal.

Make Your Own Decisions

The youth should not be excluded from decision-making

End Double Standards

We demand an end to the hypocrisy of being old enough to vote but not old enough to be voted for.

Taxation and Leadership

We are old enough to contribute taxes, so why not leadership?

Leaders of Today

The Constitution should recognize us as leaders of today, not just tomorrow.

Energy and Ideas

Malawi needs the energy and innovative ideas of youth, not leaders who retire after a term.
